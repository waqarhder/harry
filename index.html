HARRY POTTER

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling.

The story follows the life of young orphan Harry Potter, who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard.

He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes friends and enemies, learns magic, and uncovers the truth about his family and the dark wizard who killed his parents.

Over the course of the series, Harry faces numerous challenges, including battles against the dark wizard Voldemort and his followers, as he seeks to protect the wizarding world and those he loves.

The story is filled with themes of love, friendship, sacrifice, and the power of good over evil.
